### Football.csv

1. **Players**
   Names of the football players with 100 or more international caps

2. **Nation**
   Nations that the players represent recognized by FIFA

3. **Confederation**
   Players and their nations are represented by 6c ontinental confederations within FIFA
      - AFC: Asian Football Confederation
      - CAF: Confederation Africaine de Football
      - CONCACAF: Confedereation of North, Central America and Caribbean Association Football
      - CONMEBOL: Confederacion Sudamericana de Futbol
      - OFC: Oceania Football Confederation
      UEFA: Union des Associations Europeennes de Football

4. **Caps**
   Represents a player's appearance in a game at the international level

5. **Years Active**
   Years from a players debut to the last game played. Some player are still active and currently playing.

6. **Caps per Year**
   Caps divided by the years active to find the rate of caps per year.
